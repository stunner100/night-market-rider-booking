Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reschedule a booking? expand_more

Go to "My Bookings", find the booking you want to change, and click the "Reschedule" button. You can choose a new date and time from the available slots.

What is the cancellation policy? expand_more

You can cancel a booking up to 1 hour before the scheduled start time for a full refund. Cancellations made within 1 hour may be subject to a fee.

How do I change my preferred zone? expand_more

Go to specific zone setting in "Profile" page section under settings and update your preferred zone.
